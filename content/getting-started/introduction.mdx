## What is KasiPOS?

KasiPOS is a specialized retail management platform built to bridge the gap between traditional informal trade and modern digital commerce. Unlike generic POS systems, KasiPOS is architected with a deep understanding of the unique challenges faced in the Kasi economy—ranging from intermittent power supply to expensive or unreliable data connectivity.

## Offline-First Strategy

Traditional cloud-based systems fail when the internet drops. KasiPOS utilizes a local-first synchronization engine that ensures your business never stops, even during extended downtime.

## Key Features

*   **Zero-Latency Transactions:** UI interactions are handled locally, providing a sub-50ms response time for every barcode scan.
*   **Automated Financial Reporting:** Daily Z-reports and monthly P&L statements generated automatically.
*   **Multi-Device Mesh:** Devices in the same store can share data locally over Wi-Fi even without an external internet gateway.
