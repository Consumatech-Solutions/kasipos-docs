# Modules Overview

KasiPOS supports module-based extensions for custom business workflows. This section provides a high-level module map for documentation and onboarding.

## Module Map

### Checkout & Sales
Includes core checkout and sales operations: `/sale`, `/transactions`.

### Inventory & Catalogue
Product and stock management workflows: `/inventory`, `/catalogue`.

### Customers & Vouchers
Customer context and promotions: `/customers`, `/vouchers`.

### Procurement & Marketplace
Stock buying and supplier/store flows: `/buy-stock`, `/buy-stock/cart`, `/buy-stock/history`, `/marketplace`, `/marketplace/[storeId]`, `/marketplace/orders`.

### Reports
Monitoring dashboards and business insights: `/reports`.

### Settings
Operational and platform configuration: `/settings`.
