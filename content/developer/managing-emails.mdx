# Email Administration

Learn how to view and manage all sent emails on the KasiPOS Dashboard.

## View email details

See all metadata associated with an email, including the sender address, recipient address, subject, and more.

## Understand email events

Here are common events you may see:

- `bounced` — The recipient's mail server rejected the email.
- `clicked` — The recipient clicked a link in the email.
- `complained` — The recipient marked the email as spam.
- `delivered` — The email was delivered to the recipient’s mail server.
- `opened` — The recipient opened the email.
- `sent` — The email was sent successfully.

## Share email link

You can share a public link to a sent email (valid for 48 hours).

## See associated logs

View request logs to troubleshoot delivery and payload issues.

## Export your data

Admins can export emails, contacts, domains, logs, and API keys as CSV.
